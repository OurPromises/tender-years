A profound book of poems
written by late master Anshuman Dubey

Anshuman passed away at the age of Sixteen. He was suffering from an incurable Neuro Muscular Disorder. This book is an expression of Anshuman's deep thoughts and feelings which he could not express in spoken words, as he was incapacitated for a long time. He kept his spirit burning to inspire and guide us to face and overcome all the unpredictable challenges of life courageously.

The Book “TENDER YEAR'S” is a English translated version of “CHHOTI SI UMAR“.

This Book is a part of libraries of most public schools across India. This is a testament to how many People were touched with his story.

This Book is a power to make us understand that if we read these poems and grasp the gravity, we can change our life. Highly Inspirational and Motivational.We are Confident that this Book will help many to change their life, irrespective of age.
