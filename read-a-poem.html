Excerpt from Slight Age by Anshuman Dubey

When the path was not difficult;

There was no concern with the world

The mind was tensionless

How precious childhood was!

how innocent, were those moments;

And how lovely was that life-

That sky of dreams

And land of imagination-

Those moments were experienced,

Playing, the naughty tricks.

Enjoying the colorful scene,

Playing in the rain!

And swaying in the breeze

This lonely, heart laments someone-

sometime to freeze that childhood.

LIFE

Life is the only time;
That you have to spend on Earth.
So commence your action now;
And make it worth.
Life is not always there;
It is not the question of life and death-
Because that rests in His hands;
But take a vow today, that in each moment of life-
You will feel cheer and gay!

Slight Age

Under the shade of trees;
In the backyard
Playing with ease,
Those moments of mother’s love
And father’s affectionate hug,
How I would run carelessly
At one call of friends for fun.
When the curiosity arises;
The childhood unfolds yet a new leaf.

WILLPOWER

Make your willpower so strong;
That, all difficulties shy away-
Let the confidence be your companion;
So that, any trouble what may come,
Is left, with no option.
Let your determination ripe;
And courage and strength join.
Fearlessly tread ahead-
And climb each rung of success,
With patient, grit instead.
