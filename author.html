About Anshuman

He confronted the dark parts of himself and worked to banish them with enlightenment and forgiveness. His willingness to wrestle with his demons caused his angels to sing. He used pain as fuel to remind him of his strength.

What started as a tragic diagnosis turned into a great and inspirational story, all thanks to one brave teenager’s determination to leave the world a better place. At the age of eight, Anshuman Dubey was diagnosed with Chronic Inflammatory Demyelinating Polyneuropathy, or CIDP, a severe neurological disorder which is characterized by progressive weakness and impaired sensory function in the legs and arms and which occurs to one in every 100,000 people. Anshuman the brightest student in his class; he was proficient in English, Sanskrit and Hindi. He was a brave boy who, in spite of his prolonged and serious medical condition, continued his life in high spirits and left a meaningful message for others to follow:

”In the end, it’s not going to matter how many breaths you took, but how you made the most of the moments you lived.”

Anshuman, you will be missed. You will be remembered forever.

I have studied the poems “Chhoti si umar“. After Reading the poems, I felt such a creative child has been called back at such a young age."
Dr. A.P.J.Abdul Kalam
Former President of India

To Stop Isn't Why I Started

To stop isn’t why I started
I represent my dream to perfection
When I achieve it, I will be to say
Here comes my destination.

LIFE

Life is the only time;
That you have to spend on Earth.
So commence your action now;
And make it worth.
Life is not always there;
It is not the question of life and death-
Because that rests in His hands;
But take a vow today, that in each moment of life-
You will feel cheer and gay!

Slight Age

Under the shade of trees;
In the backyard
Playing with ease,
Those moments of mother’s love
And father’s affectionate hug,
How I would run carelessly
At one call of friends for fun.
When the curiosity arises;
The childhood unfolds yet a new leaf.

WILLPOWER

Make your willpower so strong;
That, all difficulties shy away-
Let the confidence be your companion;
So that, any trouble what may come,
Is left, with no option.
Let your determination ripe;
And courage and strength join.
Fearlessly tread ahead-
And climb each rung of success,
With patient, grit instead.
